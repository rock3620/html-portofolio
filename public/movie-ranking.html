The Best Movies According to GICO

My top 3 movies of all-time.

DORAEMON

This is the film that I always look forward to every Sunday morning, because it makes me daydream and imagine the story shown in the film.

SINCHAN

Tells the story of a small child with unique naughtiness but with funny elements that add to this film making it entertaining to watch.

SAINT SEIYA

This film, at the time, boasted impressive fighting scenes, delivering a thrilling experience that was always worth watching.
